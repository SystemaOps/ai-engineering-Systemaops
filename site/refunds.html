7-day money-back guarantee

If you are not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, you can request a full refund within 7 days of the purchase date.

How to request a refund

  1. Email info@systemaops.com from the address used at checkout.
  2. Include your order number (it's in your purchase receipt).

That's it. We process refund requests within 5 business days. The refund is issued to your original payment method; depending on your bank or card issuer, it may take an additional 5–10 business days to appear.

What happens after a refund

Your access to the curriculum is revoked when the refund is issued. Any license key associated with the purchase is deactivated.

Fair use of this policy

This policy exists so you can buy with confidence, not as a way to obtain the curriculum without paying. The Content is digital and delivered in full immediately on purchase, so we actively check for abuse. We may decline a refund where, for example:

  • access logs show systematic bulk-downloading, scraping, or mirroring of the Content before the refund request;
  • the same person or payment method runs repeated purchase-and-refund cycles;
  • the license key was shared or used from an unreasonable number of devices.

Copying or retaining the Content after a refund is a violation of the Terms of Service and copyright law: the refund terminates your license, and any further use or distribution of the Content is unlicensed.

Your statutory rights

This policy is in addition to, and does not limit, any rights you have under applicable consumer-protection law. By purchasing, you acknowledge that the digital content is delivered immediately and, where applicable law allows (for example the EU/UK withdrawal rules for digital content), you consent to immediate performance and waive the statutory withdrawal right; our voluntary 7-day guarantee above applies regardless.
