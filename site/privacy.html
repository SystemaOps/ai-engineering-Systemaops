Privacy Policy

Effective: June 10, 2026

Terms of Service Refund Policy Privacy Policy

The short version: this site is built to know as little about you as possible. There are no accounts, your learning progress never leaves your browser, and payment details go to our payment processor — not to us.

1. What we collect, and where it lives

Learning progress — your device only

Lesson completion, quiz answers, learning-plan settings, and theme preference are stored in your browser's local storage. This data is never transmitted to us or anyone else. Clearing your browser data erases it.

Purchases — handled by the payment processor

Checkout is operated by a third-party payment processor acting as merchant of record. They collect your name, email, billing details, and payment method under their own privacy policy (linked at checkout). We receive your email address, order details, and license status so we can grant access and provide support. We never see or store card numbers.

Hosting

The site runs on our own server. Like any web server, it records IP addresses and request metadata in standard server logs, used to deliver the site, prevent abuse, and enforce the fair-use terms of the Refund Policy. We run no page-view analytics.

Third-party resources

Pages load fonts from Google Fonts and one JavaScript library from the jsDelivr CDN. Your browser sends standard request data (such as your IP address) to those services when fetching these resources, governed by their respective privacy policies.

Email

If you email info@systemaops.com, we keep the correspondence so we can help you and maintain support records.

2. What we don't do

  • No selling or renting of personal data — to anyone, ever.
  • No advertising trackers or cross-site profiling.
  • No marketing emails unless you explicitly opt in.

3. Legal bases

Where laws such as the GDPR apply, we process purchase and support data to perform our contract with you, and minimal hosting/analytics data under our legitimate interest in operating and securing the Service.

4. Retention

Order and license records are kept as long as needed for support, tax, and accounting obligations. Support emails are kept as long as useful for assisting you. Local-storage data is under your control and stays on your device.

5. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export personal data we hold about you, and to object to or restrict processing. To exercise them, email info@systemaops.com. For data held by the payment processor, you can also contact them directly. You can erase all local data yourself by clearing this site's browser storage.

6. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their personal data.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time; the "Effective" date above reflects the latest revision. Material changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: info@systemaops.com.
